· What this is ·

The Librarians' Assembly is a community within the wider Jedi Order roleplay. We are a writing-focused guild, organised around the preservation of knowledge, the practice of teaching, and the keeping of careful record. Where other Orders train for combat, we train for the catalogue.

Membership is small and the standard high. Pledges are admitted by written application, mentored by Tutors, advanced by submission. Every keeper from Pledge to Chief Librarian holds to the same Charter. The work is patient. The welcome is real.

What follows is the Order's record of itself. Read what interests you. If you would join, the petition is at the bottom.
