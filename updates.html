A record of changes

Significant additions and changes to the Assembly's website are noted here.

· April 2026 ·

Tools, achievements, updates page

A workshop has opened. The Tools page (in the footer) holds three small instruments for the keepers: a name generator, a holocron-title generator, and a personality test that suggests what kind of keeper you might be. None are required; all are for those who would use them.

Achievements have been quietly added across the site. A small gold notice appears when one is unlocked. There is no leaderboard, no points, and most are private to your browser.

This Updates page itself was added so members may see what is new without combing through the commit log.

· April 2026 ·

A real address

The Assembly now lives at librariansassembly.org. The old GitHub Pages address still works and redirects here automatically, so no existing link is broken.

HTTPS is enforced. A Content Security Policy was added at the same time, which restricts what the visitor's browser will allow the site to load. Visitors will not notice; intruders will.

· April 2026 ·

The honoured failures

A third easter egg was added to the Path page. After a moment on the page, a hint appears at the bottom. Holding H for three seconds reveals notes from keepers who did not make it past certain ranks. The Order keeps record of its failures alongside its successes.

The Path page no longer lists the requirements next to each rank. The descriptions stand on their own. The requirements remain documented elsewhere for those who need them.

· April 2026 ·

Atmosphere and depth

Several quiet additions:

  • Pages now fade in softly instead of flashing white
  • The Charter has four scholarly footnotes attached to specific articles
  • A "currently reading" indicator appears in the topbar on long pages
  • The holocron sentinel's hover text rotates through six different teasers
  • A reading streak tracker counts your consecutive Daily Reading visits, with badges at 7 and 30 days
  • A "From the Charter" section on the homepage shows a different article each visit
  • A feather-quill icon in the topbar toggles "scribe mode", a parchment palette
  • Glossary tooltips: in-universe terms now show definitions on hover or tap
· April 2026 ·

The welcome page

A separate welcome.html page was added as a curated first impression for newcomers, intended for sharing in recruitment posts and DMs. It is deliberately not linked from the rest of the site.

· April 2026 ·

Search and structure

Site-wide search was added. A magnifying glass icon in the topbar opens it; from any page, pressing / or Ctrl+K does the same. Searches happen instantly in the browser, with no server involved.

Permalinks were added to the Charter, Order, and Apply pages, allowing direct links to specific articles, pursuits, and sections.

A sitemap and robots.txt were added so search engines and link previewers can find every page properly.

· April 2026 ·

Migration to dedicated pages

The site was restructured from a single long-scroll homepage into a deliberate gateway and nine focused pages. Each major concept now has its own home: the Order, the Path, High Command, the Charter, Petition, the Oath, Voices, the Daily Reading.

The Charter gained a marginalia easter egg, accessible by holding Shift on the page.

The homepage retained the holocron sentinel and the heretic crystal as its atmospheric pieces.
