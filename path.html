How one rises

Ten ranks across four tiers, from Pledge to Chief Librarian.

Rank in the Assembly is earned, not granted. The Path of Ascension is written down so that no advancement is mysterious and no member is left guessing what is asked of them. Read it openly. The requirements that follow are the ones that bind every keeper, applied evenly.

Low Ranks
I.
Pledge

Pledges are unofficial members of the division who have recently passed the Librarians' Assembly application. They have full access to the Librarian robes and team. Pledges have one week to achieve Student and be accepted into the Assembly as a bonafide member.

Requirements
  • Pass the application, OR be handpicked with reason by a member of High Command.
II.
Student

Students are full-fledged members of the Librarians' Assembly. The first of the low ranks, they may begin working towards advancing in the Assembly.

Requirements
  • 1 submission (one chance of revision will be granted).
  • Pass the orientation quiz.
  • Must be a member of the Consular Pathway.
III.
Librarian

Librarians are low-ranks of the Assembly who have begun to prove themselves as members. They may now gear up for the rank of Intellect.

Requirements
  • 3 submissions.
  • Attend 4 trainings.
  • Have been a Student for at least 1 day.
Middle Ranks
IV.
Intellect

Intellects are the first official middle-rank of the Jedi Librarians' Assembly. They have gained enough knowledge and maturity to co-host training sessions.

Requirements
  • 6 submissions.
  • Attend 10 trainings.
  • Attend a Patrol.
  • Attend an Advanced Introductory Lecture.
  • Attend 1 of: Seminar, Symposium, Debate, or Activity Push.
  • Have been a Librarian for at least 3 days.
V.
Tutor

Tutors are highly respected and have gained much knowledge within the Assembly. They are permitted to assist the High Ranks.

Requirements
  • 8 submissions.
  • Attend 16 trainings.
  • Co-host 5 events.
  • Attend 3 of: Seminar, Symposium, Debate, Activity Push, or Patrol.
  • Have been an Intellect for at least 5 days.
High Ranks
VI.
Lore Keeper

Lore Keepers are dedicated members devoted to the study and curation of the Jedi Archives. Strong scholars fulfilling the calling of wisdom and knowledge.

Requirements
  • 10 submissions.
  • Attend 20 trainings.
  • Co-host 8 events.
  • Attend 4 of: Seminar, Symposium, Debate, Activity Push, or Patrol.
  • Receive 2 Divisional or higher Endorsements.
  • Become Librarian of the Week at least once.
  • Pass a High Command + Archivist vote to take the Trials.
  • Have been a Tutor for at least 1 week.
  • Must be a Consular AJK.
VII.
Archivist

Archivists are the Senior High Ranks of the Assembly. Held to the highest level of critical thinking and team management, they take charge in managing both the Assembly and the High Ranks, acting as mentors to all.

Requirements
  • Have no active strikes within TJO or LA.
  • Served as a Lore Keeper for two weeks (excluding the Probationary Period and any Inactivity Notices).
  • Host 35 events.
  • Review 20 submissions.
  • Become High Rank of the Week at least three times.
  • Write a 500-word minimum essay about your journey in LA and why you have stayed for so long.
  • In the same document as your essay, write an example of an explanation of the Assembly to a recently joined Pledge.
  • Pass a unanimous High Command vote.
High Command
VIII.
Historian

The Historian is a highly respected rank within the Jedi Order, acting as a role model to those of lower rank. They assist the Curator in overseeing the High Ranks, and report directly to the Curator of the Archives and Chief Librarian.

Requirements
  • Be handpicked by the Chief Librarian and the Curator of the Archives.
IX.
Curator of the Archives

Curator of the Archives is the second-in-command of the Librarians' Assembly. This person directly oversees the High Ranks and reports to the Chief Librarian.

Requirements
  • Be handpicked by the Chief Librarian.
X.
Chief Librarian

Chief Librarian is the highest achievable rank, in first command of the Librarians' Assembly. This person oversees the Assembly, the Jedi Archives, and serves on the Council of First Knowledge.

Requirements
  • Be chosen by the Jedi High Council.
