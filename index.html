The Order

The Jedi Librarian's Assembly is a specialized collective within the Jedi Order, devoted to the preservation, organization, and dissemination of knowledge.

Based in the Great Jedi Archives on Coruscant, these Jedi serve as guardians of history, philosophy, and the teachings of the Force. Members of the assembly are scholars, archivists, and researchers, whose mission is to ensure that the wisdom of generations is accessible to all Jedi.

"What is preserved, endures.
What is taught, multiplies."
· Pursuits of the Order ·

Four disciplines form the
foundation of our Assembly.

01 · Tenet
The Archive

Texts, recordings, and oral histories are gathered, indexed, and made available to those who would learn. The archive is the heart of the order.

02 · Tenet
Instruction

From basic training to advanced symposia, members teach one another openly. No knowledge is hoarded; every Initiate may rise to Loremaster.

03 · Tenet
Discourse

Through debate and assembly, contradictions in the record are weighed and resolved. Truth is found in argument, not in silence.

04 · Tenet
Vigil

We patrol our holdings, defend our archives, and bear witness when history is being made. Knowledge unguarded is knowledge lost.
