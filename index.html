· The Order ·

The Jedi Librarian's Assembly is the keeper of the Order's record. Based in the Great Jedi Archives on Coruscant, our members are scholars, archivists, and tutors — Jedi whose blade is the index and whose meditation is the catalogue. The work is patient, the standard high, the welcome real.
